Simple Pricing Plan

Sellers - 1 ₹/Product Order - Prepaid

As a seller, you need to maintain a credit balance against your HumBhiOnline account.

You can recharge your account at any time in multiples of 1 ₹. For each rupee you recharge for, you will get a credit for 1 product order.

That is, if you recharge for 10₹ and buy 10 credits. You can receive 10 product orders through the platform. It can be in the form of 1 order with 10 products or 10 orders with one product each. When across all your customers, the number of product orders you received reaches 10, you are taken offline (i.e not suggested to customers any more) till the account is recharged. It is that simple!

On signing up, you are given a complimentary credit of 20 product orders as a sign up bonus to try out the platform for no-charge.
